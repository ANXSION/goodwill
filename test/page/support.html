Our Support

Goodwill Rehabilitation Centre recognizes the profound impact addiction has on families. Family members often experience a great deal of pain, confusion, and frustration as they witness their loved one struggle with addiction. For this reason, we believe in a holistic approach to recovery that extends beyond the individual..

At Goodwill Rehabilitation Centre, we actively involve families in many aspects of our treatment programs. These sessions provide family members with the opportunity to:

  • Gain a deeper understanding of addiction and its effects.
  • Develop healthy coping mechanisms for dealing with the challenges of addiction.
  • Learn how to best support their loved one on their recovery journey.
  • Foster stronger communication and rebuild trust within the family unit.

Our goal is to empower families to become active participants in the healing process. By working together, families can build a strong support system that fosters a brighter future for everyone involved and their loved ones. Together, we can build a brighter future free from substance abuse.
