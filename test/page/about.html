About

Goodwill Rehabilitation Centre (GRC), established in 2008 and located in Salbari Siliguri, offers comprehensive support for individuals struggling with addiction.

Our team of highly trained and experienced counselors and staff understands the challenges of addiction firsthand. Many of us have overcome personal experiences with drug addiction and alcoholism, and with the unwavering support of our families, have achieved lasting recovery. This lived experience fuels our passion to guide others toward a life free from substances.

We are committed to providing a holistic treatment approach. This includes access to on-call medical expertise and a nearby hospital to address any medical needs that may arise during recovery.

At Goodwill, we consider ourselves a family dedicated to serving our community. We extend a compassionate hand to those struggling with addiction and their loved ones. Together, we can build a brighter future free from substance abuse.

Our Vision

Goodwill Rehabilitation Centre is committed to offering hope and healing to individuals and families affected by drug addiction and alcoholism. We strive to spread a message of recovery, goodwill, and a brighter future within our community. We provide comprehensive treatment and aftercare solutions tailored to empower individuals towards a healthy, responsible, and productive life free from addiction

We understand the complexities of addiction and its related issues. Recognizing the need for ongoing development, Goodwill Rehabilitation Centre is dedicated to research and development initiatives. Through continued learning, we refine our programs to address these complex challenges most effectively. Beyond recovery, Goodwill Rehabilitation Centre champions self-sufficiency for our clients. We offer skill development opportunities, equipping individuals for a productive and responsible life after rehabilitation.

The Goodwill Society was born from a deep understanding of addiction's impact. Many of our staff members have personally experienced the pain, despair, and shame associated with addiction, alongside the struggles faced by their loved ones. However, through dedicated recovery efforts and unwavering support from their families, they have found a life free from addiction.

Driven by the desire to prevent others from enduring similar hardship, Goodwill Rehabilitation Centre extends a helping hand. We understand the fear and isolation that often accompany addiction. We want you to know there is hope. Our team is here to walk alongside you, share your burden, and fight this battle together.

Remember, you are not alone. If you or a loved one is struggling with addiction, please don't hesitate to reach out. We are just a phone call away. Contact Goodwill Rehabilitation Centre today and start your journey towards a brighter future.
